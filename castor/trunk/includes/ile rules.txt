Unary prefix operators:
     ++,--   is  T&
     !       is  bool
     +,-,~,  is  T

Unary postfix operators: 
     ++, -- is T

Binary infix operators:
     ==, <, >, <=, >=, &&, || is bool
     +,-,*,/,^,%,<<,>>,|,&    is  L

Unsupported :
     derefence operator *
     address of operator 
     member access operator ->
